About Us

We're freshly-formed team of passionate game developers, united by a shared vision: to make games that are genuinely fun..

Born from a group of university friends in 2025, we may be new to the industry, but our ambition runs deep. Every project we create is a step forward--learning, experimenting, and pushing ourselves past each challenge.

Right now, we work with free resources, fueled by creativity and determination. Your support means the world to us--whether that's donating, mentoring, or even hiring us for a project.

Until then, take a look at our recent game creations. They're a glimpse into how we work, what we imagine, and how much we love bringing ideas to life.
